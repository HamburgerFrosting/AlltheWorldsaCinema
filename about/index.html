About the Author

I'm a guy who writes reviews and other stuff about movies.

I like movies, but I feel like I never have time to watch them because of other stuff that goes on, so I made this website so I have an excuse to watch more movies.

This website talks about movies, both what I think of specific movies as well as talking about trends in movies and news related to recent movie events.

I hope you enjoy this site. If not, that's fine too.
